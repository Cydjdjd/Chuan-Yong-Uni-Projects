Introduction

The essential story being told by my website is that technology can be a very interesting topic to follow.Given the vast improvements in technology today,everyone should try to be as interested in technology as possible, to keep up with the times and to stay relevant. Hence,with my website,I try to present technology in a interesting manner that is engaging, that would interest prospective readers.I tried to implement a structure inspired by a couple of gaming websites with technology related advice put into it. The structure of homepage was inspired by a website which talked on templates we can use for webpages, whereas the articles are inspired by numerous gaming webpahes.

State 3 things that have inspired you when creating your website.

The first thing that inspired me were gaming websites.The reason why it is so inspired by gaming, is because I find that gaming websites have a very interesting format that helps to capture the interest of readers.This coupled with the interesting content of technology,creates an immersive website experience.Next, the content was inspired by technology websites across the web,as I used articles from acroos the web that are related to technology.This is to make it more original as opposed to taking content directly from Techadvisor, which is the newslatter that I am making a website for.Taking content directly from the magazine would make it too unoriginal as techadvisor already has its own website.Lastly, I was inspired by website making related websites that provided me with templates,codes and website design ideas.These website provide me with ideas and pre made structures that are easy to follow and more professional in a sense.

State 3 ways in which your site is accessible.

The website is accessible as it involves simple colours which are in this case black and white only.Therefore,this makes it easy for people to read when they are tired and also makes it simpler for people to understand especially for people that are impaired to easily use them.It is filled with animations that allow the users to easily navigate the website,ensuring that they would be able to view content and interact with the content easily. It has images with alternate text, ensuring that should images fail to load, the text acts as an alternative replacement,ensuring that people would still be able to understand. This also allows screen readers to read the images allowing visually impaired users to use the website.

State 3 ways in which you have considered usability in your site.

The website is high in usability as it is highly responsive, and is suited to be used on phones, tablets as well as laptops. It is also good in usability as it contains numerous interesting features such a comments section where you can add comments, a searchbar to search for pages, carousels and animations.This provides a highly interactive website experience that is enjoyable to the user.Lastly, another aspect that improves usability is that does not have a lot of errors as most of errors have been debugged providing a good website experience.
